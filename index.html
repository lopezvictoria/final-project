  3. ¾ cup blackberries and/or blueberries
  4. ¼ cup grated beet (from about 1 small)
  5. ¼ cup mint leaves
  6. 2 tablespoons lime juice
  7. 1 tablespoon ground chia seeds

About me

Hi, Victoria Lopez is a Fine artist who Received her BFA from Montserrat College in 2017. Victoria has learned a plethora of skills durring her time at the College, ranging from fusing in multiple forms to writing css and html. She currently takes on many freelance opportunities. She choose to create this single serving website about vegetables because why not? We could all use a little more veggies in our lives. Well thanks for reading.
